About Mighty Missions

Mighty Missions is a database of non-profits, social enterprises, and other mission-based organizations, created to faciliatate these organizations in collaborating with each other. We believe connections based on similar causes and mission will bring a greater reward and enable each organization to increase their ability to meet the needs of their communities.
